# Black Pepper Chicken Thighs with Fried Rice & Homemade Stock

[!Black Pepper Chicken](../../images/black-pepper-chicken/black-pepper-chicken-002.png)

A VaporCHEF weekend recipe that transforms one package of chicken thighs into three meals:

- Black Pepper Chicken
- Black Pepper Fried Rice
- Homemade Chicken Stock

---

# Philosophy

One package of chicken should become more than one dinner.

This recipe uses nearly every part of the chicken:

- Meat → Dinner + Fried Rice
- Bones → Stock
- Skin → Crispy Cracklings
- Fat → Cooking Fat (optional)

---

# Ingredients

## Chicken

- 2–3 lb bone-in chicken thighs
- Skin removed
- Trim excess fat

## Seasoning

- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1½ tbsp coarse black pepper
- 1 tsp MSG
- 1 tsp garlic powder

## Aromatics

- 1 large onion, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced

## Liquid

- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 cup chicken stock (or water)

---

# Pressure Cooker

1. Remove chicken skin.
2. Trim excess fat.
3. Season chicken thoroughly.

Using the Instant Pot sauté mode:

1. Brown onions.
2. Add garlic.
3. Toast additional black pepper for 30 seconds.
4. Deglaze with soy sauce, Worcestershire, sesame oil and chicken stock.
5. Add chicken.

Cook:

- High Pressure: 8 minutes
- Natural Release: 10 minutes

Remove chicken.

Reserve all cooking liquid.

---

# Dinner

Serve whole chicken thighs with:

- Rice
- Vegetables
- Spoonful of cooking broth

---

# Save for Tomorrow

Refrigerate:

- Remaining chicken
- Cooking liquid
- Bones

---

# Homemade Chicken Stock

Return to the pressure cooker:

- Chicken bones
- Reserved cooking liquid
- 1 onion (quartered)
- 2 carrots
- 2 celery stalks
- 4 garlic cloves
- 2 bay leaves
- 10–15 peppercorns
- Water to cover

Pressure Cook:

- High Pressure: 45 minutes
- Natural Release: 20 minutes

Strain.

Optional:

Reduce by half for concentrated stock.

Freeze in containers or ice cube trays.

---

# Crispy Chicken Skins

Pat dry.

Season:

- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic Powder
- Pinch of MSG

Bake:

400°F

25–35 minutes

or

Air Fry:

375°F

10–15 minutes

Serve as a snack or crumble over fried rice.

---

# Black Pepper Fried Rice

Ingredients

- Day-old rice
- Diced black pepper chicken
- Eggs
- Onion
- Frozen peas/carrots
- Soy sauce
- Sesame oil
- MSG
- Green onions
- Fresh cracked black pepper
- 2–3 tbsp homemade chicken stock

Cook:

1. Chicken fat or oil
2. Eggs
3. Onion
4. Vegetables
5. Rice
6. Chicken
7. Soy sauce
8. Homemade stock
9. Sesame oil
10. MSG
11. Fresh black pepper

Finish with:

- Green onions
- Crispy chicken skin

One package of chicken becomes:

- Dinner

Minimal waste.
Maximum flavor.
